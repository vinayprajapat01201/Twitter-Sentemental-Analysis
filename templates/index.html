Twitter Sentiment Analysis

Twitter Sentiment Analysis is the process of analyzing tweets on Twitter to determine the sentiment or opinion expressed within them. It involves using natural language processing, machine learning, and text analysis techniques to classify tweets as positive, negative, or neutral. This analysis can provide valuable insights into public opinion, customer feedback, and trends in various industries. By understanding the sentiment of tweets, businesses, researchers, and individuals can make informed decisions and take appropriate actions.
